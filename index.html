IoT

IoT means Internet of Things which basically includes every device that is connected to the internet. For example if a IoT device communicates with a smartphone via Bluetooth it makes using the device much more convenient an easier to use. There are already many household appliances like Washing- or Coffee-Machines. Which means that you could make your Coffee only by Pushing a single Button on your Smartphone.

Why is IoT important?

Almost like the Industrialization IoT completely revolutionizes the way we think about technology. It helps you to solve many problems, which were simply too complex before. It will totally change how we interact with Things. A few years ago, the only purpose of the internet was to connect people with each other and for sharing information’s between Humans, so it was the “Internet of People”. But since our PCs and Phones getting smarter and more powerful year by year, you could call the internet now the “Internet of Things”. They also download, generate, process and upload data automatically. In a modern smart Phone there are many sensors that are telling you about the brightness, the GPS position, Gyroscope, microphone, distance sensors, some of the data collected by the sensors are being sent to a server, which processes the information. And because every Smartphone is connected via WIFI or LTE you hardly ever do not have an internet connection. Because of that you can use your smartphone as a System.

What does IoT devices make so Smart?

Because of the mostly enormous amount of IoT devices that are connected to the Internet. A massive load of data is being generated which can be processed by an algorithm which mostly consists of a GPU driven AI. Which is Learning from this Information. For example, all weather stations are connected to the internet to make the weather forecasts more precise and reliable.
